One robot, four superpowers

CarthaBot turns screen time into maker time with a complete learning journey.

Build

Turn ideas into real structures — snap on chassis, wheels and accessories, and make CarthaBot yours.

Connect

Link sensors, lights and friends — kids learn how networks and teams work by connecting things together.

Code

From colourful event blocks at age 4 to real Python at 14 — logic and problem-solving grow with your child.

Learn

Experiment and discover — science, technology and engineering through play, missions and challenges.

Four moods, one happy robot

The CarthaBot Companion app

Everything your child needs to bring CarthaBot to life — one playful app, no account, in French and English.

Coding for under-6s

Pair picture events with actions — "when I hear a clap → turn green!" — and press play.

3D playground & missions

Test programs on a virtual CarthaBot first — six worlds, coins, missions and stars to earn.

Draw with your hands

Sketch in the air in front of the webcam — CarthaBot picks up a pen and draws it on paper.

Claps & sounds

CarthaBot has real ears — clap your hands and it reacts, with a built-in microphone test.

Wireless coding

Send programs over Wi-Fi or Bluetooth — no cable chase, the robot roams free.

Blocks to Python

CarthaSoft block coding for beginners, a real Python editor for big kids — one smooth ramp.
